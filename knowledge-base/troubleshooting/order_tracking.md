# Order Tracking — Troubleshooting Guide

## Step 1: Check Your Confirmation Email
A shipping confirmation email with a tracking link is sent within 24 hours of dispatch. Check your spam and promotions folders if you cannot find it. The subject line is: *"Your [Company] order has shipped — Order #XXXXXX"*.

## Step 2: Allow Time for Tracking to Activate
Carrier tracking systems can take 12–24 hours to update after a label is created. If the tracking page shows "Label created" or "Awaiting carrier pickup", wait until the next business day before contacting support.

## Step 3: Identify the Carrier
The carrier name and tracking number are in your shipping confirmation email. Common carriers used:
- **DHL Express** — for express and international orders
- **FedEx Ground/Home** — for domestic standard orders
- **USPS Priority** — for lightweight domestic orders

Enter the tracking number directly on the carrier's website for the most up-to-date status.

## Step 4: Common Status Messages and What They Mean

| Status | Meaning | Action |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Label Created | Label printed; parcel not yet scanned by carrier | Wait 24 hours |
| In Transit | Moving through carrier network | No action needed |
| Out for Delivery | With the delivery driver today | Ensure someone is available |
| Delivery Attempted | Driver visited but could not deliver | Check carrier site for redelivery options |
| Exception / Delay | Carrier has flagged an issue | Contact support |
| Delivered | Carrier marks as delivered | See "Package marked delivered but not received" below |

## Package Marked Delivered But Not Received
1. Check with neighbours and any safe-drop locations noted by the driver.
2. Allow 24 hours — carriers occasionally mark packages delivered one day early.
3. If still missing after 24 hours, contact support with your order ID. We will open a carrier investigation (typically resolved within 3–5 business days).

## Order Not Arrived Within Expected Window
Standard shipping is 5–7 business days; express is 2–3 business days. If your order has not arrived within **10 business days** of the dispatch date, contact support. We will either re-ship or refund depending on the outcome of the carrier investigation.

## International Orders
Customs clearance can add 2–7 business days beyond the estimated delivery date. Customs fees or import duties, if applicable, are the customer's responsibility.

## Contact Support
Have the following ready:
- Order ID (format: ORD-XXXXXXXX)
- Tracking number
- Description of the issue
