# Privacy Policy

## What Data We Collect
We collect and process personal data strictly to provide and improve our services. Data categories include:

| Category | Examples | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Identity | Full name, username | Account creation, order management |
| Contact | Email, phone, shipping address | Order delivery, customer support |
| Payment | Card type, last 4 digits, billing address | Transaction processing (full card numbers are never stored by us) |
| Device / Usage | IP address, browser type, pages visited | Security, fraud prevention, service improvement |
| Order history | Products purchased, dates, amounts | Support, returns, warranty validation |

## How We Use Your Data
- **Order fulfilment:** processing, shipping, and delivery of purchases
- **Customer support:** resolving issues, verifying identity
- **Fraud prevention:** detecting and blocking unauthorised transactions
- **Service improvement:** aggregated analytics (never linked to an individual without consent)
- **Legal obligations:** complying with applicable laws and regulations

## Data Sharing
We do **not** sell personal data to third parties. We share data only with:
- **Payment processors** (e.g., Stripe) — for authorising transactions; they are PCI-DSS compliant
- **Shipping carriers** (e.g., DHL, FedEx) — name and address for delivery
- **Analytics providers** — anonymised/aggregated data only
- **Law enforcement** — only when required by a valid legal order

## Data Retention
| Data type | Retention period |
|---|---|
| Account data | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Order records | 7 years (legal/tax requirement) |
| Support tickets | 3 years |
| Browsing / analytics | 13 months (rolling) |

## Your Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction you have the right to:
- **Access** a copy of the personal data we hold about you
- **Correction** of inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Deletion** ("right to be forgotten") — subject to legal retention requirements
- **Portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Withdraw consent** for marketing communications at any time
- **Object** to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, contact our privacy team via the support portal. We will respond within **30 days** (as required by GDPR / CCPA).

## Security
Personal data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Access is restricted to staff who need it to perform their role. We conduct annual security audits and penetration tests.

## Cookies
We use strictly necessary cookies (session management) and, with consent, analytics and preference cookies. You can manage cookie preferences at any time via the cookie banner or your account settings.

## Contact
For privacy-related concerns or data requests, contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@[company].com or submit a request through the support portal.
