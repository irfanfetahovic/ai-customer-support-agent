# Shipping Information (FAQ)

We provide both standard and express shipping options.

Estimated delivery times:
- Standard shipping: typically 5–7 business days
- Express shipping: usually 2–3 business days

Orders are generally processed within 24 hours of placement. However, during peak periods, processing may take up to 48 hours.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. Please allow up to 24 hours for tracking updates to appear.

In rare cases, delays may occur due to:
- carrier issues
- customs processing (for international orders)
- high seasonal demand

If your package has not arrived within 10 business days, please contact support.
