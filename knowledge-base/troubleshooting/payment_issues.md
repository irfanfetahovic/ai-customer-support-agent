# Payment Issues — Troubleshooting Guide

## Payment Declined at Checkout

### Step 1: Check Your Card Details
Ensure the following are entered exactly as they appear on your card:
- Card number (no spaces)
- Expiry date (MM/YY)
- CVV / security code (3 digits on back for Visa/Mastercard; 4 digits on front for Amex)
- Billing address matches what your bank has on file

### Step 2: Check for Sufficient Funds
Some banks place a temporary authorisation hold even on declined transactions, which can temporarily reduce your available balance. Log in to your banking app to check.

### Step 3: Try a Different Payment Method
We accept:
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- PayPal
- Apple Pay / Google Pay (on supported browsers/devices)
- Shop Pay / Afterpay (where available)

If one card fails, try a different card or payment method before contacting support.

### Step 4: Contact Your Bank
Banks often block first-time purchases from new merchants or large orders as fraud prevention. Call the number on the back of your card and ask them to authorise the transaction. Then retry checkout.

### Step 5: Browser / Device Issues
- Clear browser cookies and cache, then retry
- Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers and VPNs, which can interfere with payment processors)
- Try on a different device or network

## Duplicate Charge
If you see two charges for the same order:
1. Check your bank statement — one charge may be a pending authorisation that will drop off within 3–5 business days.
2. If both charges are fully settled, contact support with your order ID and the dates/amounts of both charges. We will investigate and refund any duplicate within 5–7 business days.

## Charged But No Order Confirmation
1. Check your spam folder for an order confirmation email.
2. Log in to your account and check order history.
3. If no order appears within 30 minutes of the charge, contact support. We will either confirm the order was created or process a full refund.

## Temporary Authorisation Holds
When you attempt a payment, a temporary hold may appear on your account for the full order amount. If the payment ultimately fails or is cancelled, this hold is automatically released by your bank, typically within 3–7 business days depending on your bank's policies.

## Accepted Currencies
All transactions are processed in USD. If your card is denominated in another currency, your bank will apply its prevailing exchange rate and may charge a foreign transaction fee.

## Contact Support
If none of the above resolves the issue, contact support and include:
- The error message shown at checkout (screenshot if possible)
- The last 4 digits of the card used
- Your order ID (if an order was created)
