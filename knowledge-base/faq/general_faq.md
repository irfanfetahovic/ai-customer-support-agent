# General FAQ

## Orders

**How do I place an order?**
Browse our website, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can check out as a guest or create an account for faster future purchases and order tracking.

**Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?**
Orders can be modified or cancelled within **1 hour** of placement, before they enter processing. After that, the order cannot be changed. If the order has already shipped, you can return it under our 30-day return policy once you receive it.

**Do I need an account to order?**
No — guest checkout is available. However, an account lets you track orders, view order history, save addresses, and manage warranty claims more easily.

**Where can I find my order ID?**
Your order ID (format: ORD-XXXXXXXX) is in your order confirmation email, subject line: *"Your [Company] order confirmation."* You can also find it in your account under **My Orders**.

## Payments

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. Shop Pay and Afterpay are available on eligible orders.

**Is my payment information secure?**
Yes. We use industry-standard TLS encryption for all transactions. Full card numbers are never stored on our servers — payment processing is handled by our PCI-DSS compliant payment partner.

**Can I pay in instalments?**
Afterpay is available at checkout for eligible orders, allowing you to split the cost into 4 interest-free fortnightly payments.

## Shipping

**How long will my order take to arrive?**
Standard shipping: 5–7 business days. Express: 2–3 business days. Overnight: next business day (order before 2 PM EST). See the full Shipping Policy for international options and costs.

**Do you ship internationally?**
Yes — to Canada, the UK, the EU, and Australia. International shipping takes 7–14 business days (standard) or 3–5 business days (express). Import duties are the customer's responsibility.

**Can I change my delivery address?**
Address changes are possible only before dispatch. Contact support immediately. Once shipped, you must contact the carrier directly.

---

## Returns and Refunds

**What is your return policy?**
30 days from the date of delivery for most products. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Digital products are non-refundable once accessed.

**How do I start a return?**
Contact support to receive a return authorisation and prepaid shipping label. Ship the item back, and your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days of us receiving and inspecting it.

**When will I receive my refund?**
Refunds are returned to the original payment method. Allow 5–7 business days for processing after we receive your return, plus your bank's own processing time (typically 3–10 business days for card refunds).

## Account and Privacy

**How do I create an account?**
Click **Sign In** on the website and select **Create Account**. Enter your name, email, and a password.

**How do I delete my account?**
Submit a data deletion request through the support portal or email privacy@[company].com. We will delete your account and associated personal data within 30 days, subject to legal data retention requirements.

**What personal data do you collect?**
Name, contact details, payment information (card type and last 4 digits only), order history, and usage data. Full details are in our Privacy Policy. We never sell your data.

## Products

**What warranty comes with my product?**
All hardware products include a 12-month limited manufacturer warranty. See the Warranty Policy and Warranty FAQ for details.

**My product is defective. What should I do?**
Contact support with your order ID and a description of the defect. If it is within the 12-month warranty period, we will arrange a replacement or repair at no cost to you.

**Do you sell replacement accessories (straps, ear tips, charging cables)?**
Yes — replacement accessories are available in our online store. Search for your product name in the accessories section.
