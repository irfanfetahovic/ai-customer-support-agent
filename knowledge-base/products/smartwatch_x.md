# Smartwatch X — Product Guide

## Overview
Smartwatch X is our flagship wearable designed for everyday health monitoring, fitness tracking, and smart notifications. It pairs with both Android (8.0+) and iOS (14+) devices via the HealthSync app.

## Technical Specifications
- Display: 1.4" AMOLED, 454×454 px, always-on option
- Battery: Up to 10 days (normal use), 5 days (always-on display), 25 hours (GPS mode)
- Charging: Magnetic snap charger; full charge in approximately 1.5 hours
- Water resistance: IP68 (up to 50 m, 30 minutes)
- Sensors: Heart rate (optical PPG), SpO2, accelerometer, gyroscope, barometric altimeter, GPS/GLONASS
- Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.0, Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n
- Storage: 4 GB onboard (music and app cache)
- Compatibility: Android 8.0+, iOS 14+

## Key Features

### Health & Fitness
- Continuous 24/7 heart rate monitoring with irregular-rhythm alerts
- Blood oxygen (SpO2) spot measurements and overnight tracking
- Sleep tracking: deep, light, REM stages with nightly score
- Stress level monitoring via heart rate variability (HRV)
- 100+ workout modes with auto-detection for running, cycling, and swimming
- Built-in GPS for route mapping without a phone
- Monthly fitness summaries in the HealthSync app

### Smart Features
- Notifications: calls, texts, emails, and app alerts (requires paired phone)
- Contactless payments via the built-in NFC chip (HealthSync Pay)
- Voice assistant integration: Google Assistant and Siri
- Weather forecasts (requires internet via paired phone)
- Alarm, timer, stopwatch, and calendar reminders

## Warranty
Smartwatch X is covered by a 12-month limited manufacturer warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. Physical damage, water damage beyond rated limits, and unauthorised modifications are not covered. See the Warranty Policy for full terms.

## Common Setup Issues
- **App not finding the watch:** Enable Bluetooth and location permissions for HealthSync; restart both devices.
- **Watch not syncing:** Force-quit the app, open it again, and pull down to refresh.
- **GPS takes long to lock:** Move outdoors to open sky; first-time GPS lock can take up to 2 minutes.

For detailed troubleshooting, see `troubleshooting/smartwatch_x_troubleshooting.md`.
