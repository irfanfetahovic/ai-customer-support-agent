# Refund Policy (FAQ)

We offer a 30-day refund policy for most physical products. This means you have 30 days from the date of delivery to request a refund.

To be eligible:
- The item must be unused and in its original packaging
- Proof of purchase is required

Refunds are typically processed within 5–7 business days after the returned item has been received and inspected.

Please note:
- Digital products (e.g., downloadable content, subscriptions) are generally non-refundable once accessed
- Items marked as “final sale” cannot be refunded

If your item arrives damaged or defective, you are eligible for a full refund or replacement. In such cases, return shipping costs are covered by us.

For more details, refer to our Returns Policy.
