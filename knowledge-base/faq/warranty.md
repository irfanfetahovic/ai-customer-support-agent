# Warranty FAQ

> **Scope:** This document covers warranty coverage, warranty claims, and product replacements only.
> For shipping questions, see `shipping_policy.md`. For payment issues, see `payment_issues.md`. For returns, see `returns.md`.

## How long is the warranty on your products?
All hardware products include a **12-month limited manufacturer warranty** from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship.

## What is covered under warranty?
Manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal use conditions. For example: a watch display that stops working, earbuds where one side produces no audio, or a battery that fails to hold any charge from the day of purchase.

## What is NOT covered?
- Physical damage (cracked screens, dents, broken charging ports)
- Liquid damage beyond the product's rated IP/IPX level
- Normal wear and tear (battery capacity degradation after heavy use, surface scratches)
- Damage from misuse or accidents
- Unauthorised repairs or modifications

## How do I make a warranty claim?
1. Contact warranty support and describe the manufacturing defect clearly.
2. Provide your proof of purchase (order confirmation email or receipt).
3. Our warranty team will assess eligibility.
4. If approved, you’ll receive a prepaid warranty return label.
5. Once we receive and inspect the device, we’ll ship a replacement or arrange a repair (typically within 5–7 business days of receipt).

## Do I have to send the product back first?
Yes. For most warranty claims, we need to inspect the defective product before issuing a replacement. We cover the return delivery cost for valid warranty claims.

## What if I no longer have the original packaging?
Original packaging is not required for warranty claims, but you should pack the product securely to avoid transit damage. We are not responsible for damage caused by inadequate return packaging.

## Will my replacement be a brand-new unit?
Replacements may be certified refurbished units of equal or greater specification. They are tested to the same standards as new units and carry the remainder of your original warranty or 90 days from shipment, whichever is longer.

## Can I extend my warranty?
Yes. A 12-month warranty extension is available for purchase within 30 days of your original purchase date. See your account dashboard or contact warranty support.

## My product broke just after the 12-month warranty expired. Can you help?
We consider out-of-warranty goodwill cases individually, particularly for defects that appear to be manufacturing-related. Contact warranty support and we will review your case, though we cannot guarantee a resolution outside the warranty period.

## Is my warranty valid if I bought the product second-hand?
The warranty is tied to the original purchase date, not the current owner. Second-hand buyers can use the warranty if it has not expired, but must provide the original proof of purchase.
