# Wireless Earbuds Pro — Product Guide

## Overview
Wireless Earbuds Pro are our premium true-wireless earbuds featuring active noise cancellation (ANC), transparency mode, and high-resolution audio. Compatible with any Bluetooth device; full feature set available via the SoundSync app (iOS and Android).

## Technical Specifications
- Driver: 10 mm dynamic driver with graphene-coated diaphragm
- Frequency response: 20 Hz – 20 kHz
- Bluetooth: 5.2, codec support: AAC, SBC, aptX, aptX HD
- ANC: Hybrid feedforward + feedback, up to -35 dB attenuation
- Microphones: 3 per earbud (ANC + call quality)
- Battery — earbuds: 8 hours (ANC off), 5.5 hours (ANC on)
- Battery — charging case: additional 16 hours, total ~24 hours (ANC off)
- Fast charge: 15 minutes → ~3 hours playback
- Charging: USB-C (case); wireless Qi charging also supported
- Water resistance: IPX5 (earbuds only; case is not water-resistant)
- Fit: 3 ear-tip sizes included (S, M, L silicone)

## Battery Life

| Mode | Playback time |
|---|---|
| Earbuds — ANC off | **8 hours** |
| Earbuds — ANC on | **5.5 hours** |
| Earbuds + case — ANC off | **~24 hours total** |
| Earbuds + case — ANC on | **~18 hours total** |

Fast charge: **15 minutes** in the case gives approximately **3 hours** of playback.
The charging case itself charges via USB-C and also supports wireless Qi charging.

## Key Features

### Audio
- Active Noise Cancellation (ANC): reduces ambient noise by up to 35 dB
- Transparency mode: lets ambient sound in for situational awareness
- Spatial audio with head tracking (iOS 15+ and Android 12+ via SoundSync)
- Customisable EQ presets in the SoundSync app

### Calls & Controls
- Dual-mic call clarity with wind-noise reduction
- Touch controls: tap, double-tap, triple-tap, long-press (fully remappable in app)
- Voice assistant: "Hey Google" and "Hey Siri" support

### Connectivity
- Multipoint pairing: connect to 2 devices simultaneously and switch seamlessly
- Auto-pause when an earbud is removed; auto-play when reinserted
- Find My Earbuds via SoundSync app (plays a tone from the earbuds)

Wireless Earbuds Pro are covered by a 12-month limited manufacturer warranty. Physical damage, liquid damage beyond IPX5 rating, and unauthorised modifications are excluded. See the Warranty Policy for full terms.

## Common Issues
- **One earbud not working:** Place both earbuds in the case for 10 seconds, then remove.
- **Poor call quality:** Clean the microphone meshes gently with a dry brush.
- **ANC not working well:** Check ear-tip fit; a poor seal reduces ANC effectiveness significantly.
