# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Destinations
We ship to all 50 US states, **Canada**, the **United Kingdom (UK)**, **European Union (EU)** member states, and **Australia**. Some remote areas may require additional transit time.

## Shipping Options and Costs

Prices and delivery times for **standard**, **express**, and **overnight** domestic shipping, plus **international shipping** rates:

| Option | Estimated Delivery | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Standard (domestic US) | 5–7 business days | Free on orders over $50; $4.99 below |
| Express (domestic US) | 2–3 business days | $12.99 |
| Overnight (domestic US) | Next business day | $24.99 |
| Standard International | 7–14 business days | From $14.99 (varies by destination) |
| Express International | 3–5 business days | From $29.99 (varies by destination) |

## Order Processing
- Orders placed before **2 PM EST on a business day** are processed the same day.
- Orders placed after 2 PM EST or on weekends/public holidays are processed the next business day.
- Processing time is separate from shipping time. Total time = processing time + transit time.

## Tracking
- A shipping confirmation email with a tracking link is sent once your order is dispatched.
- Allow up to 24 hours for the tracking link to become active after the email is sent.
- Track your order at any time via your account dashboard under **My Orders**.

## Signature Requirements
Orders over $150 may require a signature on delivery. If no one is available, the carrier will leave a notification card with redelivery or pickup instructions.

## P.O. Boxes
Standard USPS shipping is available for P.O. boxes. Express and overnight services are not available for P.O. box addresses.

## International Shipping — Important Notes

**Do you ship internationally?** Yes — **international shipping is available.** If you are wondering "do you ship to Canada?", "do you ship to the UK?", "do you ship to Europe?", or "do you ship internationally?" — yes, we do.

We ship internationally to **Canada**, the **United Kingdom (UK)**, **EU countries** (France, Germany, Netherlands, Spain, Italy, and others), and **Australia**.

- **Customs and import duties:** Any customs fees or import duties charged by the destination country are the customer's sole responsibility. These are not included in the shipping fee and we cannot predict their amount.
- **Customs delays:** International orders may be held by customs for 2–7 additional business days. This is outside our control.
- **Restricted items:** Some products may not be available for international shipping due to export regulations.

## Address Changes
Address changes can only be made before the order is dispatched. Contact support immediately if you need to change a delivery address. Once dispatched, we cannot redirect parcels — you will need to contact the carrier directly.

## Failed Deliveries and Returns to Sender
If a parcel is returned to us because of an incorrect address or failed delivery attempts, we will contact you to arrange re-shipment. A re-shipping fee may apply.

## Lost or Damaged in Transit
If your order arrives damaged or is confirmed lost by the carrier, contact support within 14 days of the expected delivery date. We will re-ship or refund at our discretion after investigating with the carrier.
