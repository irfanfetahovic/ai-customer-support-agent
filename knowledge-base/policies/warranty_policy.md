# Warranty Policy

## Coverage
All hardware products sold by us include a **12-month limited manufacturer warranty** from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use conditions.

## What Is Covered
- Manufacturing defects (e.g., faulty display, non-functional buttons, battery that does not hold charge from day one)
- Hardware failures not caused by physical damage or misuse
- Components that fail under normal operating conditions within the warranty period

## What Is NOT Covered
- Physical damage (cracked screens, dents, broken ports)
- Liquid damage beyond the product's stated IP/IPX rating
- Damage from misuse, neglect, or accidents
- Unauthorised modifications or repairs
- Normal wear and tear (scratches, faded coatings, degraded battery capacity after 500+ charge cycles)
- Loss or theft
- Cosmetic damage that does not affect function

## How to Make a Warranty Claim
1. Contact customer support and describe the defect.
2. Provide proof of purchase (order confirmation email or order ID).
3. Our team will assess whether the fault is covered under warranty.
4. If approved:
   - For minor defects: we may provide a repair service or replacement part.
   - For significant defects: we will ship a replacement unit once the defective unit is received.
5. Return delivery for valid warranty claims is covered by us. A prepaid return label will be emailed to you.

## Replacement Units
Replacement units may be refurbished products of equal or greater specification. They carry the remainder of the original warranty period, or 90 days from the replacement date — whichever is longer.

## Extended Warranty
An optional 12-month warranty extension is available for purchase within 30 days of the original purchase date. Contact support or visit the warranty extension page in your account dashboard.

## Warranty and Statutory Rights
This warranty does not affect your statutory rights under applicable consumer protection law. In some jurisdictions (e.g., EU member states) you may have additional rights beyond those stated here.

## Serial Number Requirement
Warranty claims require the product serial number. For Smartwatch X it is printed on the back of the watch case. For Wireless Earbuds Pro it is printed inside the charging case lid.
