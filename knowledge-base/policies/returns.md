# Returns and Refunds Policy

Our return policy allows customers to return eligible products within thirty (30) days from the date of delivery.

Return eligibility requires:
- The product must be unused
- It must be returned in original packaging
- All included accessories must be present

To initiate a return:
1. Contact customer support
2. Receive a return authorization and shipping instructions
3. Ship the item using the provided return label

Refund timeline:
- Inspection: 2–3 business days after receipt
- Refund processing: 5–7 business days

Exceptions:
- Digital products are not eligible for refunds once accessed
- Personalized or custom-made items are non-refundable

If a product is defective or damaged upon arrival, customers are entitled to a full refund or replacement. In such cases, return shipping costs are covered.

This policy should be read together with the FAQ Refund Policy, as minor differences in wording may apply depending on context.
