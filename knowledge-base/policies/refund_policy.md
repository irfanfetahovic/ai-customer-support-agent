# Refund Policy

## Eligibility
- Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase.
- Annual plans are prorated after the first 14 days.
- Refunds are not available for one-time setup services once delivery is complete.

## Verification Steps
1. Verify customer identity and account email.
2. Confirm purchase date and payment method.
3. Check whether previous refund exceptions were granted.

## SLA
- Initial response target: under 4 business hours.
- Refund decision target: under 2 business days.

## Escalation
Escalate to a human billing specialist when:
- fraud indicators are present,
- chargeback already started,
- or policy exceptions are requested.
